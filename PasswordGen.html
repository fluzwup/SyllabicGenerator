Random password generator

This program generates random passwords based on a provided pattern. The passwords are composed of randomly selected syllables (S), symbols (_), and digits (n), assembled in the sequence provided. The default pattern, SSS_nnn, will provide over 2^44, or 17 trillion possible combinations. Unlike generators that produce random strings of letters, digits, and symbols in any order, this method produces passwords that are to some degree pronounceable, making them easier to memorize.
